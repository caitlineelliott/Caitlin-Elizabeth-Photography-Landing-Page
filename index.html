My name is Caitlin Elliott and I am so excited to capture your big day! The whole wedding process is so special to me, and I love being able to come alongside couples, learn their stories, and help document this important season.

I've been a professional photographer for 9 years, and, in that time, I've had the privilege of photographing weddings all over the world. Some of my favorite destination weddings were in the Great Smoky Mountains, Edinburgh, and Riviera Maya!

My team has grown from just me and my camera to a staff of 10 photographers in our collective. Because we all work together under one umbrella, we're able to capture all the facets of your day in perfect detail. We can't wait to work with you!
